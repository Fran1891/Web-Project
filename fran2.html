About Us

Doña Juana Empanadas started in the city with a simple dream: to make the best homemade empanadas. Our recipe is a family tradition, using fresh ingredients and lots of love.

Our Mission

Our goal is to deliver authentic, traditional empanadas. Made with care and high-quality ingredients, every bite feels like homemade goodness.

Our Values

Quality: We use only fresh, high-quality ingredients.

Tradition: We respect and honor the family recipe that defines us.

Commitment: We work every day to deliver a product our customers enjoy and appreciate.
